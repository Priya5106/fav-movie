  4. Sirai

    5. Sirai is a 2025 Indian Tamil-language crime drama directed by debutant Suresh Rajakumari and produced by Seven Screen Studio. Written by Tamizh (of Taanakkaran fame) and based on his real-life police experiences, the film stars Vikram Prabhu as a dedicated police constable tasked with escorting a murder suspect, played by newcomer LK Akshay Kumar, from prison to court. Featuring a soulful score by Justin Prabhakaran, the movie explores the shifting dynamics between the law and the accused during their long journey, ultimately exposing systemic corruption and the "circus of power" within the judicial system.
